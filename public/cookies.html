Cookie Policy

Effective Date: May 16, 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. They help improve your browsing experience.

How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics Cookies: Collect information about how visitors use the site to help us improve.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings.

Cookies We Use

Below is a list of cookies that Coptic Guide currently uses, along with simple explanations for each:

Cookie Name What It Does (Simple Explanation)
__cflb Helps protect the site from bots and spam, like a security guard checking visitors.
h-captcha-response Stores your answer to the "I'm not a robot" test, so the website knows you passed.
hcaptcha Remembers if you recently passed the bot test, so you don’t have to do it again soon.
cf_clearance Confirms your browser is safe and trusted to visit the site, acting like a visitor badge.
__cfduid Identifies trusted visitors and blocks harmful ones, but doesn’t track what you do.
__cf_bm Cloudflare cookie to detect bots and protect against suspicious activity.
cf_ob_info Helps Cloudflare optimize delivery of the site, improving your experience.
cf_use_ob Works with cf_ob_info for better website performance under Cloudflare's service.

Your Cookie Choices

You can control or disable cookies via your browser settings. Note that disabling some cookies may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party services to set cookies on our site for analytics or advertising purposes. These third parties have their own privacy policies.
